More About Lori
Lori is the president and founder of Extra Mile Marketing. She is an award-winning industry expert on the subject of marketing, with more than 25 years of experience as a senior marketing executive for corporations and advertising agencies. Lori offers significant expertise in Business-to-Business and technology marketing, along with consumer products and retail. She specializes in developing strategic marketing plans that encompass training, consulting and full marketing plan execution. Extra Mile Marketing serves national and international clients including: Microsoft, Symantec, Rackspace, T-Mobile, The Ford Motor Company, Unified Logic, Lakeview Financial, and others. Lori has a special interest in the needs of children and has served on several boards including the Bellevue School District’s Strategic Planning and Math Adoption committees and served as Board Chairman and Board Development Chair for Friends of Youth, a large non-profit serving children and youth in the greater Puget Sound area. She earned her B.A. in Communication Studies at the University of California, Santa Barbara, but is happy to call the Seattle area home. Lori enjoys her family, travel, golf, bridge, and wine tasting.
More About Homa

Her research experience includes studying the effects of natural products and synthetic analogs on prominent molecular targets in prostate cancer cell models, as well as the action on their phenotype. As the Administrative Assistant for EMM, she applies her high attention to detail and organizational skill sets to every project in our fast-paced environment. With the ability to balance multiple projects at once, Homa handles a multitude of correspondences, reporting, document creation, event planning, and project-based assistance with efficiency and accuracy. In her spare time, Homa tutors high school and middle school students in the sciences and mathematics. When she is not working or tutoring, you can find Homa exploring new things around the Seattle Metropolitan area.
