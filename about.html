ABOUT EMM

EXTRA MILE MARKETING IS A SMALL BUSINESS WITH A LARGE IMPACT.



Extra Mile Marketing is a full-service, strategic marketing agency specializing in strategy, consulting, training, and tactical execution of marketing and messaging plans that maximize sales and return on investment. We offer marketing support to national and international companies to help create clear, concise messaging that connects employees, customers, and partners worldwide. Our team has specific expertise in a variety of industries, bringing best practices from technology, finance, professional services, healthcare, manufacturing, telecom, retail/grocery and more.

We offer significant experience in sales channels and partner programs and we're happy to share our insights. We've consulted with over 7,000 companies in more than 25 countries, and we've trained hundreds of companies on new ways to grow their business. For over a decade, our proven channel partner programs have focused on the recruitment, engagement, and retention of strong partners.

Our clients will all agree that we are more than just a marketing agency, but also their partner. Creative, smart, fun, collaborative, trustworthy, experienced, reliable, generous, and flexible all describe our approach and company culture.

Our Nine Values for Driving Success


Creative
We are committed to uncovering creative solutions to help our clients achieve their goals. We foster an open environment that includes collective brainstorming resulting in these creative options being delivered to our clients.
Smart
Our vast experience and knowledge are applied to every project, and we strive to find the right solution for our clients. We use a methodical approach, from start to finish, for every project.
Fun
We use interactive elements to get people engaged. We use humor to build relationships. We celebrate personal and professional success.
Collaborative
We collaborate on all projects internally, with our clients, and with vendors. We believe the success of our projects is dependent on the success of our collaboration.
Trustworthy
We are committed to what we say. We believe in high integrity. Our client relationships are cultivated with trust, and, as a result, our clients return.
Experienced
We have worked in partnership with over 7,000 technology companies. Our team members have an average of over 20 years of marketing experience. Our well-rounded team is proficient in sales, marketing, project management, communications, and technology.
Reliable
Our clients receive their completed projects on time, within budget, and beyond expectation. We eliminate obstacles and find a solution, providing ease of mind for our clients.
Generous
We have a generous spirit and think of others. We give-back through charitable contributions. We strive go above and beyond the scope to make our clients the hero.
Flexible
We foster a “Yes, if - No, but” environment to accommodate the circumstances of our client’s needs. We have a love of learning and are always open to new ideas. We find alternative solutions, when necessary, in our scope of work allowing us to meet the needs of our clients.
