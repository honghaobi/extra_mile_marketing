WHO WE ARE


Extra Mile Marketing is a full-service strategic marketing firm that has been helping technology companies and their Partners reach their target markets with marketing and sales support for over 14 years. Our team also has expertise in a variety of other industries, bringing best practices from finance, professional services, healthcare, manufacturing, telecom, and retail/grocery, offering a unique approach so your messaging stands out in a crowded marketplace.

We specialize in helping you attract the RIGHT prospects and produce high-quality, engaging, and interactive content that markets your solutions and service offerings to key audience members. How can EXTRA MILE MARKETING help you?

WHAT WE DO


Strategic Marketing Planning

No marketing campaign should start without first setting the stage for your company and your offerings.  We work with you to develop effective messaging and marketing plans that makes you stand out.

Sales and Marketing Enablement

Your Sales & Marketing teams are only as good as the tools and resources you provide. We offer a unique approach so your sales and marketing materials stand out in a crowded marketplace. 

Customer/Partner Experience

Your customers and partners are the pinnacle of your success. Our in-depth knowledge enables us to create successful and impactful websites, microsites, and launch activities.

Go-to-Market Campaigns

You need a variety of ways to reach out to your customers and prospects.  We specialize in creating strong Go-To-Market campaigns tailored to your specific industry or market segment, ensuring you’re reaching your target.
